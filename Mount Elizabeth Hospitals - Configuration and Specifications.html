Data Storage Information

  • NAS: 8TB total capacity (~6TB usable)
  • Thin slice data (Cardiac/Colon): Export snapshot to PACS
  • Thin/Thick slice non-cardiac/colon: Direct storage
  • Image volume growth: 3% per year (3-5 year projection)

Deployment Strategy

Thin slice DICOM data is sent to the VIMS Server (thick slice typically sent to PACS), which processes it and builds a 3D volume, then copies the DICOM data and the 3D volume to the VitreaAdvanced storage.

Users access this information via VitreaCore or VitreaAdvanced sessions where they may create snapshots and/or batches, which are saved to the VitreaAdvanced DICOM storage and may be exported to PACS.
