Product Lifecycle Overview

In the ever-changing world of healthcare technology and information security, Canon Medical continues to advance our products to align with industry changes and to introduce new features to our product portfolio. With each product comes a product lifecycle.

Understanding and knowing the dates of this lifecycle will help you make informed decisions about when to upgrade, and help you realize the full value of your Canon solution.

Policy Information: Canon Medical is committed to communicating major milestones for our products. We encourage all customers to be aware of the product lifecycle and end-of-life (EOL) policy for older versions of the Vitrea products, and to upgrade to a newer, fully supported version to take advantage of new feature enhancements and fixes to known issues. Current versions of our products have enhanced security and are deployed on supported hardware.

End-of-Life Definitions

Understanding what each EOL stage means for your Vitrea Advanced Visualization software.

Current Version

These product versions are available for sale and distribution, and eligible for product support and maintenance. They are also eligible for software updates at the discretion of Canon Medical.

End of Sale

These product versions are no longer available for sale or distribution. They are eligible for product support and maintenance. Re-distribution of software is permitted, unless prohibited due to regulatory reasons.

End of Support Life

These product versions are no longer available for sale, distribution, or eligible for product support and maintenance. They are not eligible to be re-distributed or installed/re-installed by Canon Medical Support.

End of Product Life

These product versions are no longer available for sale, distribution, or eligible for product support, maintenance, re-distribution or software updates.

Note: In most cases, End of Support Life and End of Product Life would occur at the same time.

Recommended Actions

It is recommended to upgrade to the latest supported version of Vitrea Advanced Visualization to keep your software supported and up to date. To learn more about the benefits of the latest version, and compare versions of Vitrea Advanced Visualization, access the What's New online comparison tool.

With Active Maintenance Contract

Canon Medical customers that are covered under a maintenance contract are entitled to a release that is UDI compliant. This is part of the existing maintenance contract.

Note: New Vitrea software may not be compatible with your existing hardware and may require the purchase of new hardware along with Canon Medical's Professional Services.

Upgrade to the latest version by contacting the Canon Medical Informatics Support team.

Without Active Maintenance Contract

If you are not currently covered under a maintenance contract, consider renewing for a UDI compliant revision of software and maintenance support.

Renew your maintenance contract by contacting the CMI Support team.

Contact Canon Medical Systems Corporation for a one-time upgrade for users that do not have a maintenance contract but would like to upgrade to the latest version.

Partner products are not included.
