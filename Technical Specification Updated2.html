CPU Calculation: Required MHz ÷ CPU Speed (MHz) = vCPUs (round up)
Example: 18,000 MHz ÷ 2400 MHz = 7.5 → 8 vCPUs

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I plan my upgrade?

We recommend planning your upgrade at least 3-6 months before the end-of-life date to allow time for testing and validation.

Will my current workflows be affected?

Vitrea 7.16 will continue to function after the end-of-life dates, but you won't receive security updates or technical support for the outdated components.

What are my upgrade options?

Canon offers several upgrade paths depending on your current configuration. Contact our sales team for a personalized upgrade assessment.

How long does an upgrade typically take?

Most upgrades can be completed in 1-2 days, but this depends on your system complexity and the need for data migration.
